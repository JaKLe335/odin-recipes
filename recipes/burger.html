Descirption

The burger is a proud American food. Though it does come from Germany. It is a meat sandwhich that is grilled usually at barbeques.

Ingredients

  • Burger Patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickle
  • Bacon
  • Bun
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Cook burger on grill
  2. Add cheese
  3. Cook and add bacon
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickle
  5. Add Condiments
  6. Place on bun
