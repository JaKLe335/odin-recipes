Descirption

Spaghetti is a dish that has spaghetti, meatballs, and sauce. It is very decilcious. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground Meat
  • Tomato
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Brown meat in pan
  2. Stir in onions till softens
  3. Add garlic, tomato paste, and spices
  4. Add water and tomatoes
  5. Let simmer for 25 minutes
  6. Cook spaghetti in pot of water for 10 minutes
  7. Drain spaghetti
  8. Add sauce
